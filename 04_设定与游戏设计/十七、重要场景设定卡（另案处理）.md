## 十七、重要场景设定卡（另案处理）

### 场景A：1640年除夕夜，白玉山棱堡顶

> 雪幕之下，整个旅顺像沉睡的钢铁巨兽。从白玉山顶望去，东港停泊的舰队像七颗暗红的炭火，西港的钟楼准时敲响零点，连绵的灯火沿着元老大道铺开两公里，金州方向的探照灯一明一灭——前线在向辽南军政委员会问安。

```
A breathtaking nighttime panorama from the ramparts of a hilltop fortress, 
overlooking a vast 17th century industrial harbor city in winter, snow falling silently. 
The city spreads below in a glittering grid of lights — countless small oil lamps in windows, 
gas-style street lamps along the main avenues, the warm glow of fires in industrial chimneys. 
Two harbors flank the city: ships' lanterns reflected in dark water. 
A distant searchlight beam sweeps from a far mountain ridge (the Jinzhou defensive line). 
A lone elder Senator figure in a navy greatcoat stands by a parapet, smoking a cigar, 
brass telescope at his side. 
Snow drifts on cannon barrels. 
A clock tower in the city below begins to strike midnight. 
Style: cinematic nocturnal panorama, deep blue-violet sky with warm urban glow below, 
sense of empire-building at its quiet peak, melancholy power.
```

### 场景B：金州地峡前线，清军主力压境

> 望远镜里，雪原尽头出现了黑色的潮水——皇太极的两白旗骑兵正在十里外集结，旌旗如林。南关堡的电报机滴滴答答，前线指挥官的指令一行一行打印出来："各炮位准备，全线进入一级战备。轻便铁路向前线运输弹药专列出发。"

```
A vast 17th century battlefield panorama from inside a star-fort observation cupola, 
winter daylight, snow-covered plains stretching to the horizon. 
Through the cupola's viewing slit: the enemy approaching — endless ranks of Manchu Eight Banner cavalry 
in winter cloaks, their banners (White Banners) flapping in the wind, 
estimated several thousand troops in formation about 5km away. 
In the foreground: artillery crews loading large cannons in concrete emplacements, 
a telegraph operator at a desk receiving urgent messages, 
an officer with a brass map case dictating orders. 
A narrow-gauge train visible to the rear bringing ammunition cars. 
Style: tense dramatic illustration, contrast between cold disciplined modernity (inside the fort) 
and traditional cavalry warfare (outside), 
sense of impending decisive battle, late Ming meets early industrial military.
```

### 场景C：东港，黄昏，"威远"舰返港

> 西边天际烧得通红，海面被反射成熔金色。"威远"号的烟囱拖着长长的黑烟，绕过黄金山的炮台，缓缓滑入老虎尾水道。岸上的归化民水兵已经在码头列队，何鸣站在司令楼的露台上，远远地举起手敬礼。

```
A 19th-century-style steam-and-sail warship entering a fortified harbor at sunset, 
17th century China, winter. 
The warship "Weiyuan" (iron-banded wooden hull, three masts with reduced winter sailcloth, 
single tall funnel belching coal smoke) glides past Golden Hill fortress 
through the narrow harbor channel. 
The sky is blazing orange-crimson, sea reflects molten gold. 
On the dock ahead: lines of naturalized-citizen sailors in navy uniforms standing at attention. 
A figure (Admiral He Ming) in a heavy navy greatcoat saluting from a fortress balcony, 
brass telescope at his side. 
Snow on the surrounding mountains, ice fragments glinting in the harbor. 
Style: cinematic naval homecoming illustration, intense warm-cold contrast, 
romantic-yet-disciplined naval atmosphere, anachronistic technology in historical setting.
```

### 场景D：净化营冬夜，新移民的第一夜

> 风雪从西港的方向卷上来，把净化营隔离营房的木窗摇得咯吱响。屋里挤着五十多人，刚剃了头、洗了澡、换上发给的棉衣，端着热气腾腾的米粥。识字员站在木箱上，慢慢念着："元老院的规矩，第一条……"

```
Interior of a refugee processing barracks at night, 17th century China, winter storm outside. 
A long brick-walled room with a central wood stove glowing warm, 
about 50 refugees of all ages sitting on wooden bunks, all freshly washed and in new padded coats, 
each holding a bowl of steaming rice porridge. 
A young naturalized-citizen instructor in a clean dark uniform stands on a wooden crate reading 
aloud from a blue-covered manual ("Senate Rules and Regulations"). 
Oil lamps hanging from the ceiling, frost on the windows. 
Through the windows: blowing snow and the distant glow of harbor lights. 
A guard in a winter cloak stands quietly by the door. 
A blackboard with chalked Chinese characters on one wall. 
Style: humanitarian historical illustration, warm interior amid cold outside, 
sense of dignified institutional welcome, organized but humane processing of refugees.
```

### 场景E：铁山高炉夜班

> 高炉口喷出炽白的铁水，火光把围着的钢铁工人脸照得通红。蒸汽鼓风机像巨兽呼吸，整个车间被低沉的节奏填满。轮班的工长拍了拍年轻归化民学徒的肩："看好了，这就是元老院给辽南打下江山的家伙。"

```
Interior of a 17th century blast furnace foundry at night, China, winter. 
A massive iron-cased blast furnace tapping molten iron — brilliant white-orange stream pouring 
into a sand mold trough, illuminating the entire workshop. 
Workers in heavy leather aprons and protective hoods stand around with long iron tools. 
A huge steam-driven blower visible to one side, flywheel turning, leather belts running. 
Anachronistic safety equipment: leather goggles, protective gloves. 
A senior foreman with an experienced look putting his hand on a young apprentice's shoulder. 
Steam, smoke, sparks fill the air; the workshop's distant rafters lost in darkness. 
Style: dramatic industrial-romance illustration in the spirit of 19th-century industrial paintings, 
warm orange-amber dominant palette, sense of a foundational moment of industrialization.
```
